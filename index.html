Engineering Foundation

Built on Real Physics

The simulator isn't a mock-up — it's a genuine thermal model grounded in fluid dynamics and heat transfer equations.

Q = ṁ · Cₚ · ΔT

Where Q = heat removed (watts), ṁ = mass flow rate, Cₚ = specific heat of coolant, and ΔT = temperature differential across the loop.
